What is Sapphire?

      Sapphire Clothing is a clothing brand founded in early 2010 by Herold Frank, unlike most fashion brands, Sapphire focuses more on character in their clothes, rather than genuine look. The main store is located in the outskirts of Sudbury, Ontario where Frank was born and raised.

At Sapphire Colthing we have highly trained professional staff. We offer both clothing for men and women in all sorts of varieties (hoodies, sweatshirts, t-shirts, etc.). You can shop sapphire online, or find us in store. Keep up with our latest releases and sales on our social medias! Listen to what our amazing customers have to say about our clothes. If you have any more questions or want to learn more about Sapphie Clothing, contact us!
